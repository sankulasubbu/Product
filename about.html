Who We Are

AMSPRO Lifesciences is a biotechnology-driven manufacturing company specializing in the development and production of probiotic solutions for human health.

Built on strong microbiology expertise and advanced fermentation technologies, we support customers through contract manufacturing, private labeling, technical support and customized probiotic solutions.

To become a globally trusted biotechnology company delivering innovative probiotic solutions that improve health and quality of life while setting new standards in scientific excellence and manufacturing.

Our Mission
